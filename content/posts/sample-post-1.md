Microsegmentation is a security technique that divides a network into small zones to prevent lateral movement...

This approach is essential for Zero Trust architectures and works hand-in-hand with WAAP solutions.

**Key Benefits**:
- Reduced attack surface
- Containment of breaches
- Granular policy enforcement
